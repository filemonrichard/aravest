The Problem

A system designed to
keep farmers poor.

Tanzanian smallholder farmers are productive. They are not lazy. But they operate inside a system with no margin for error — no credit when inputs are needed, no insurance when rains fail, no market access when harvest comes. Every year, the cycle resets at zero.


ARAVEST was built because this is not an agricultural problem. It is a structural problem. And structural problems require systemic solutions.

80%

of farmers are excluded from formal credit — not because they are risky, but because no one has ever measured their risk properly.

30%

of crops lost annually to climate events that satellite monitoring could predict days in advance.

3–5x

markup on inputs through fragmented supply chains and layers of intermediaries eating into farmer margins.

The Solution

One system.
Everything a farmer needs.

ARAVEST integrates five services into a single closed-loop platform accessible via USSD — no smartphone, no internet connection required. Farmers dial in. The system works.

💳

Input Financing

Seeds, fertilizer, and tools financed upfront. Repayment automated at harvest through mobile money.

🛰

Satellite Monitoring

AI-powered farm monitoring using satellite imagery to track crop health, weather risk, and productivity.

🌧

Climate Insurance

Weather-index insurance that pays out automatically when satellite data confirms a climate event.

📱

Agronomic Advisory

Weekly SMS and USSD guidance in Swahili, tailored to the farmer's crop, region, and current conditions.

🤝

Market Access

Direct connections to verified buyers and structured crop markets. No middlemen. Better prices.

How It Works

From registration
to harvest.

01

Farmer Registers

Dials *ARAVEST# on any phone. Completes a simple onboarding in Swahili. No smartphone required.

02

Farm Profile Created

AI and satellite imagery generate a farm risk and productivity profile. No paperwork. No branch visit.

03

Inputs Delivered

Credit approved and inputs delivered through verified suppliers. Farmer starts the season strong.

04

Season Monitored

Satellite tracks the farm weekly. Insurance activates if needed. Advisory SMS arrives every week.

05

Harvest and Repay

Crops sold to verified buyers via the ARAVEST marketplace. Loan repaid automatically. Farmer keeps the profit.
